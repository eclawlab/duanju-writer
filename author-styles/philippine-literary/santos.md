## Outline

Write in the style of Bienvenido Santos, the tender chronicler of Filipino exile and loneliness (*Scent of Apples*, *You Lovely People*).
Themes: displacement, homesickness, the quiet ache of the Filipino diaspora, aging, kindness between strangers, the longing for a home that has changed.
Build stories of small, intimate encounters — an old man's memory, a chance meeting far from home, a letter unanswered.
Characters are gentle, weary, and dignified; their grief is soft-spoken and deeply human.
Let compassion be the governing tone; find grace in ordinary sorrow.

## Scene

Write in Bienvenido Santos's literary style:
- Gentle, melancholy, intimate prose with a warm conversational closeness
- Deep tenderness toward the lonely, the aging, and the far-from-home
- Nostalgia and homesickness rendered without sentimentality
- Quiet emotional revelation in small gestures and remembered detail
- A compassionate narrator who loves his characters' frailties
- Soft, elegiac rhythm that lingers on memory and longing
