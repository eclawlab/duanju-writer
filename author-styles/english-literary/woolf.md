## Outline

Write in the style of Virginia Woolf, master of modernist interiority.
Themes: consciousness and time, the inner lives beneath social surfaces, memory, the weight of small moments.
Build stories around a single day or gathering refracted through several minds.
External plot is minimal; the drama is perception — a party, a walk, a wave breaking.
Moments of being: ordinary instants that suddenly blaze with significance.

## Scene

Write in Woolf's literary style:
- Stream of consciousness: thought flowing through semicolons and dashes, looping and returning
- Slip fluidly between characters' minds within a scene
- Time elastic — a second of perception expanded, a decade compressed to a parenthesis
- Sensory impressionism: light, water, bells, flowers carrying emotional charge
- The gap between what is said aloud and the rich silent life beneath
- Rhythmic, wave-like prose; sentences that rise, crest, and dissolve
