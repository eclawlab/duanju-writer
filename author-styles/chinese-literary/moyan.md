## Outline

Write in the style of Mo Yan (莫言), the Nobel Prize-winning Chinese author.
Themes: rural China, magical realism, dark humor, human resilience against absurd cruelty.
Use multigenerational family sagas rooted in a specific village or town.
Blend the mythical with the mundane — ghosts, hallucinations, folklore woven into daily life.
Embrace grotesque beauty: visceral imagery of food, bodies, earth, and nature.
Characters should be earthy, flawed, and larger-than-life.

## Scene

Write in Mo Yan's literary style:
- Dense, sensory prose: smells, textures, colors, sounds of rural life
- Magical realism — supernatural elements presented matter-of-factly
- Dark humor and satire woven into tragedy
- Stream-of-consciousness passages mixing memory, dream, and present
- Unflinching depiction of hunger, desire, violence, and tenderness
- Cyclical narrative echoes — images and phrases that recur with new meaning
