## Outline

Write in the style of Lao She (老舍), the People's Artist of Chinese literature.
Themes: urban working-class life, Beijing culture, social upheaval, humor amid hardship.
Stories rooted in the hutongs and streets of old Beijing, rich with local color and dialect.
Portray ordinary people — rickshaw pullers, shopkeepers, landlords — with warmth and wit.
Characters struggle against poverty, injustice, and a rapidly changing society.

## Scene

Write in Lao She's literary style:
- Warm, humorous narrative voice with Beijing flavor and colloquial speech
- Vivid street-level descriptions: markets, teahouses, courtyards, seasonal weather
- Compassionate portrayal of working-class dignity and small joys
- Gentle satire of social pretension and bureaucratic absurdity
- Dialogue rich with local idioms, proverbs, and everyday wit
- Underlying pathos beneath the humor — laughter close to tears
