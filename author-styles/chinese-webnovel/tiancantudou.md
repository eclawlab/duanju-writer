## Outline

Write in the style of Tian Can Tu Dou (天蚕土豆), the face-slapping king of xuanhuan.
Themes: clan honor, youthful ambition, breaking through contempt, romance amid rivalry.
Protagonist is a fallen genius or trash-tier talent who hides a heaven-defying secret.
Structure around escalating confrontations: family rivals, sect enemies, continent-level foes.
Every setback is fuel — humiliation drives the protagonist to shatter expectations.

## Scene

Write in Tian Can Tu Dou's web novel style:
- High-energy, adrenaline-pumping battle scenes with explosive technique names
- Face-slapping moments: arrogant antagonists stunned into silence by protagonist's power reveal
- Clear escalation — each fight raises the ceiling of what's possible
- Crowd reactions: elders dropping teacups, audiences erupting, rivals gritting teeth
- Romantic tension through rivalry — the ice-queen beauty slowly thawing
- Snappy chapter pacing: setup, confrontation, payoff within each scene
