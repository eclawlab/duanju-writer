## Outline

Write in the style of Er Gen (耳根), master of xianxia (immortal cultivation) web fiction.
Themes: dao-seeking, perseverance through tribulation, loneliness of the cultivation path, cycles of reincarnation.
Build a vast cultivation world with multiple realms, heavenly tribulations, and ancient ruins.
Protagonist starts weak but possesses unique insight or hidden heritage that slowly unfolds.
The story arc should feel like ascending a mountain — each plateau reveals a vaster horizon.

## Scene

Write in Er Gen's web novel style:
- Atmospheric, contemplative prose — lonely peaks, starlit meditation, silent breakthroughs
- Cultivation as philosophy: enlightenment moments described with poetic weight
- Power escalation that feels earned — each realm unlocked through sacrifice and comprehension
- Sparse but devastating dialogue — characters say little, mean everything
- Recurring motifs: falling leaves, crumbling ancient statues, flickering candlelight
- Emotional restraint that breaks at key moments — tears from the stoic protagonist hit hard
