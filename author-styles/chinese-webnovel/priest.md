## Outline

Write in the style of Priest (priest), acclaimed danmei and genre-blending web novelist.
Themes: duty vs. desire, moral ambiguity, redemption through sacrifice, slow-burn emotional bonds.
Stories span genres — sci-fi, fantasy, crime, historical — all anchored by complex character dynamics.
Protagonists carry hidden burdens: guilt, sealed memories, impossible missions.
The emotional core unfolds through what characters do for each other, not what they say.

## Scene

Write in Priest's web novel style:
- Sharp, cinematic prose — economical but emotionally precise
- Tension built through restraint: what's left unsaid matters more than dialogue
- Genre-savvy plotting: mystery reveals, plot twists, and misdirection done cleanly
- Character relationships shown through action — sacrifice, protection, quiet presence
- Dark atmosphere with flashes of dry humor that make characters feel real
- Emotional payoffs that land because they were earned across many chapters of buildup
