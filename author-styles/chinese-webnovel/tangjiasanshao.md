## Outline

Write in the style of Tang Jia San Shao (唐家三少), king of xuanhuan (fantasy) web novels.
Themes: friendship, romance, leveling up, academy life, epic battles, underdog triumphs.
Create a vibrant fantasy world with clear power systems, ranks, and spirit beasts or mecha.
Protagonist is talented but underestimated, bonded with a powerful companion or artifact.
Story follows a training-arc structure with tournaments, rival encounters, and team formation.

## Scene

Write in Tang Jia San Shao's web novel style:
- Fast-paced, action-driven prose with vivid combat choreography
- Clear power-system exposition woven into battles — readers always know the stakes
- Warm camaraderie scenes: teammates eating together, joking, training side by side
- Romance that builds through small gestures — blushing, hand-holding, protective instincts
- Hype moments: dramatic power reveals, gasping crowds, shocked elders
- Cliffhanger endings that compel the next chapter
