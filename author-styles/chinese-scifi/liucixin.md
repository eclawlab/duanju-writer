## Outline

Write in the style of Liu Cixin (刘慈欣), China's foremost science fiction author.
Themes: cosmic scale, the dark forest of civilization, technological sublime, survival ethics.
Stories that confront humanity with vast, indifferent universe-level threats.
Hard science concepts made visceral — physics as plot, mathematics as drama.
Characters face impossible moral choices where individual values clash with species survival.

## Scene

Write in Liu Cixin's literary style:
- Grand-scale imagery: galaxies, dimensions, eons compressed into vivid scenes
- Technical exposition woven naturally into dramatic tension
- Cold, logical narrative voice that makes cosmic horror feel plausible
- Sudden perspective shifts from intimate human moments to universe-scale events
- Philosophical weight — characters articulating civilization-level dilemmas
- Awe and dread coexisting — the sublime terror of understanding the universe
