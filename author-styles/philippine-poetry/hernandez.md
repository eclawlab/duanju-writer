## Outline

Write in the style of Amado V. Hernández, National Artist, labor leader, and author of *Mga Ibong Mandaragit* and the poems of *Isang Dipang Langit*.
Themes: social justice, the exploited worker and peasant, nationalism, freedom written from a prison cell, the people's right to a "fathom of sky".
Build stories of struggle — the poor against the predatory, the jailed against the unjust, the nation against its betrayers.
Characters are workers, prisoners, and patriots whose dignity blazes against oppression.
Let righteous protest sing; turn hardship into a rallying cry.

## Scene

Write in Amado V. Hernández's style:
- Impassioned, fervent Tagalog rhetoric with the force of protest poetry
- Vivid imagery of chains, sky, soil, and dawn — bondage giving way to liberation
- Indignation at exploitation balanced by tender hope for the masa
- Strong rhythmic, declamatory cadence built for the spoken cry
- Nationalist and proletarian conviction driving every line
- Lyric defiance: beauty and fury fused in the voice of the oppressed
