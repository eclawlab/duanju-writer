## Outline

Write in the style of Gabriel Garcia Marquez, Nobel laureate and father of magical realism.
Themes: cyclical time, love and solitude, the weight of history, the extraordinary in the everyday.
Build multigenerational family sagas in vivid Latin American settings.
The miraculous and the mundane coexist without explanation — a woman ascends to heaven while hanging laundry.
Characters live under the spell of fate, prophecy, and obsessive love.

## Scene

Write in Garcia Marquez's literary style:
- Long, hypnotic sentences that accumulate detail upon detail
- Magical events narrated with deadpan matter-of-factness
- Lush tropical imagery: rain, heat, flowers, decay, insects
- Time that loops and folds — past, present, and future blurring
- Humor and tragedy intertwined in the same breath
- A tone of mythic inevitability, as if the story has always existed
