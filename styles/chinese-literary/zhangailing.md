## Outline

Write in the style of Zhang Ailing (张爱玲), master of modern Chinese literary fiction.
Themes: doomed romance, decaying aristocracy, female psychology, the cruelty of love and time.
Set stories in 1930s-40s Shanghai or Hong Kong — a world of cheongsams, mahjong, and fading grandeur.
Focus on intimate domestic dramas where small gestures carry enormous emotional weight.
Characters are sharp-witted, self-aware, yet trapped by desire and social convention.

## Scene

Write in Zhang Ailing's literary style:
- Exquisite, precise prose with striking visual metaphors and color imagery
- Psychologically penetrating — characters dissected with cool, ironic clarity
- Sensory details of clothing, interiors, food, and weather as emotional mirrors
- Dialogue that hides more than it reveals, charged with subtext
- A melancholy, fatalistic tone — beauty always shadowed by decay
- Cinematic scene composition: light, shadow, and framing used deliberately
