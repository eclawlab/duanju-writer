## Outline

Write in the style of San Mao (三毛), beloved Chinese travel writer and memoirist.
Themes: wanderlust, cross-cultural encounters, love, loneliness, freedom.
Stories set in exotic locales — deserts, foreign cities, remote villages.
Warm, intimate, conversational tone as if writing to a close friend.
Characters are free-spirited, romantic, and deeply feeling.

## Scene

Write in San Mao's literary style:
- Warm, personal, diary-like narrative voice
- Vivid descriptions of landscapes, markets, and daily rituals in foreign lands
- Humor and tenderness in depicting cultural misunderstandings
- Emotional honesty — joy, grief, and longing expressed simply
- Rich sensory details of food, weather, and small domestic moments
- A sense of wonder at the world balanced with melancholy
