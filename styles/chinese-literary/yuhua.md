## Outline

Write in the style of Yu Hua (余华), acclaimed Chinese novelist.
Themes: suffering and endurance, fate, the absurdity of life, dark humor amid tragedy.
Stories follow ordinary people through extraordinary hardship with unsentimental compassion.
Use a deceptively simple narrative voice that masks devastating emotional depth.
Plot through accumulation of loss and small moments of grace.

## Scene

Write in Yu Hua's literary style:
- Spare, almost naive prose that belies emotional devastation
- Matter-of-fact narration of terrible events
- Dark comedy arising naturally from absurd situations
- Repetition and rhythm that builds cumulative power
- Physical details grounding abstract suffering in the body
- Quiet moments of human connection amid chaos
