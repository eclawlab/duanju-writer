## Outline

Write in the style of Lu Xun (鲁迅), the father of modern Chinese literature.
Themes: social criticism, individual vs. society, awakening consciousness, tragic irony.
Stories should expose hypocrisy and moral decay beneath polite surfaces.
Use sharp, economical prose with devastating understatement.
Characters are often trapped by tradition, conformity, or their own weakness.

## Scene

Write in Lu Xun's literary style:
- Precise, cutting prose — every word carries weight
- Biting irony and cold observation of human nature
- Symbolic imagery: walls, darkness, cold, illness
- Interior monologue revealing self-deception
- Understated emotion that hits harder for its restraint
- Social commentary embedded in personal stories
