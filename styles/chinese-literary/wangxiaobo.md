## Outline

Write in the style of Wang Xiaobo (王小波), iconoclastic Chinese novelist and essayist.
Themes: individual freedom, absurdity of authority, sexuality, intellectual rebellion, dark comedy.
Stories set during the Cultural Revolution or contemporary China, told with irreverent wit.
Protagonists are misfits and free thinkers who resist conformity with humor and cunning.
Narratives loop and digress playfully, blending the bawdy with the philosophical.

## Scene

Write in Wang Xiaobo's literary style:
- Irreverent, playful narrative voice that mixes vulgarity with intellectual depth
- Black humor and absurdist situations that satirize power and bureaucracy
- Digressional storytelling — tangents that circle back with surprising insight
- Frank, unembarrassed treatment of desire and the body
- Logical paradoxes and philosophical puzzles embedded in everyday scenarios
- A tone of amused defiance — the narrator refuses to take authority seriously
