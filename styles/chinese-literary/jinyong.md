## Outline

Write in the style of Jin Yong (金庸), master of wuxia (martial arts) fiction.
Themes: honor, loyalty, love vs. duty, the jianghu (martial world), political intrigue.
Create a rich martial arts world with rival sects, hidden techniques, and legendary weapons.
Interweave romance, brotherhood, betrayal, and redemption.
Characters grow through suffering and martial arts training.

## Scene

Write in Jin Yong's literary style:
- Vivid, choreographed martial arts sequences with poetic names for techniques
- Rich world-building: inns, mountain temples, secret caves, bustling markets
- Dialogue that reveals character allegiance and hidden motives
- Balance of humor, romance, and high-stakes combat
- Classical Chinese idioms and references woven into modern prose
- Cliffhangers and dramatic reveals
