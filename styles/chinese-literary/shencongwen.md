## Outline

Write in the style of Shen Congwen (沈从文), lyrical chronicler of rural Hunan.
Themes: pastoral innocence, the beauty of simple life, first love, nature and humanity intertwined.
Set stories along rivers, in border towns, among ethnic Miao and Tujia communities of western Hunan.
Celebrate the purity and dignity of rural people untouched by urban corruption.
Stories move at the pace of river water — gentle, inevitable, deeply felt.

## Scene

Write in Shen Congwen's literary style:
- Lyrical, painterly prose — landscapes rendered like watercolor scrolls
- River imagery: flowing water as metaphor for time, love, and fate
- Tender, unhurried depiction of customs, festivals, and daily rituals
- Characters who express emotion through action and silence, not words
- A nostalgic, elegiac tone — beauty always on the edge of being lost
- Nature and human feeling mirroring each other seamlessly
