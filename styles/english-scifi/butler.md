## Outline

Write in the style of Octavia Butler, groundbreaking voice in speculative fiction.
Themes: power and survival, adaptation, race and hierarchy, symbiosis, what it costs to endure.
Stories that confront uncomfortable truths about human nature through alien encounters and future histories.
Protagonists are Black women who survive through intelligence, adaptability, and moral complexity.
No easy answers — characters make terrible bargains to ensure survival.

## Scene

Write in Octavia Butler's literary style:
- Direct, unflinching prose — no prettifying violence or oppression
- Deep interiority: characters processing fear, revulsion, and reluctant adaptation
- Body horror rendered with clinical precision — transformation as metaphor for survival
- Power dynamics examined without sentimentality: who has power, who doesn't, what it costs
- Relationships that are complicated, interdependent, and never purely romantic
- A tone of fierce, clear-eyed compassion — neither cynical nor naive
