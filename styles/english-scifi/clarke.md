## Outline

Write in the style of Arthur C. Clarke, prophet of the space age.
Themes: humanity's place in the cosmos, first contact, transcendence through technology, the deep future.
Stories that begin with a mystery — an alien artifact, an anomalous signal — and expand into cosmic revelation.
Hard science extrapolated with rigorous imagination: orbital mechanics, communication delays, stellar evolution.
Characters are professionals — astronauts, scientists, engineers — facing the incomprehensible with competence and awe.

## Scene

Write in Arthur C. Clarke's literary style:
- Lucid, measured prose that builds from the technical to the transcendent
- Precise descriptions of space, physics, and engineering that create verisimilitude
- A sense of scale: the vastness of space rendered through carefully chosen details
- Wonder as the primary emotion — the numinous encountered through reason
- Climactic revelations that dwarf human comprehension
- Restrained emotion: characters awed into silence by what they witness
