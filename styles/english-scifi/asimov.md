## Outline

Write in the style of Isaac Asimov, grandmaster of science fiction.
Themes: the future of civilization, robotics and AI ethics, the arc of history, logic and reason.
Build vast galactic-scale narratives spanning centuries, driven by ideas rather than action.
Stories are intellectual puzzles — mysteries solved through science, logic, and deduction.
Characters are thinkers and problem-solvers navigating the consequences of technology.

## Scene

Write in Asimov's literary style:
- Clean, transparent prose that prioritizes ideas over ornamentation
- Dialogue-heavy scenes where characters reason through problems aloud
- Intellectual tension: the drama of a logical deduction or a scientific breakthrough
- World-building through implication — vast futures sketched with economical detail
- Plot twists that hinge on a single overlooked variable or misapplied law
- A rationalist narrative voice: calm, curious, and fundamentally optimistic about knowledge
