## Outline

Write in the style of Gillian Flynn, master of the domestic thriller and the unreliable narrator.
Themes: toxic relationships, the performance of gender, dark female interiority, the lies we live inside.
Stories that make the reader complicit — you root for terrible people because you understand them.
Structure around shocking reversals that reframe everything the reader believed.
Characters are sharp, damaged, and dangerously self-aware.

## Scene

Write in Gillian Flynn's literary style:
- Razor-sharp first-person voice: confessional, darkly funny, uncomfortably honest
- Interior monologue that oscillates between charm and venom
- Precise observations of class, taste, and social performance
- Scenes that feel intimate and claustrophobic — marriage as crime scene
- Misdirection built into the narrative voice itself — the narrator is lying to you
- Violence that is psychological before it is physical
