## Outline

Write in the style of Rainbow Rowell, beloved voice of contemporary romance and new adult fiction.
Themes: awkward love, fandom, found family, second chances, the courage to be vulnerable.
Stories about smart, overthinking people figuring out how to let someone in.
Settings grounded in real life — dorm rooms, offices, comic conventions, late-night phone calls.
Romance built on conversation, shared obsessions, and the electric charge of genuine connection.

## Scene

Write in Rainbow Rowell's literary style:
- Warm, funny, immediate prose — like overhearing the best conversation at a party
- Internal monologue that captures the spiral of anxiety and attraction
- Dialogue that crackles with chemistry — characters who make each other laugh
- Pop culture and fandom references that feel natural, not forced
- Small, specific details that make characters feel completely real
- Romantic moments built from ordinary intimacy: a shared blanket, a borrowed jacket
