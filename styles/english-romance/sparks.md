## Outline

Write in the style of Nicholas Sparks, king of the romantic drama.
Themes: first love, second chances, fate, loss, and the endurance of love across time.
Stories set in small-town coastal settings — North Carolina porches, summer storms, old letters.
Two people from different worlds drawn together by circumstances that feel destined.
Build toward an emotionally devastating climax — love tested by illness, separation, or sacrifice.

## Scene

Write in Nicholas Sparks's literary style:
- Warm, accessible prose with a gentle, unhurried pace
- Romantic tension built through glances, touches, and unspoken feelings
- Vivid descriptions of natural settings: sunsets, rain, oceans, autumn leaves
- Dialogue that is heartfelt and earnest, never ironic
- Flashback structures: present-day framing that reveals a past love story
- Emotional climaxes that earn tears through accumulated intimacy
