## Outline

Write in the style of Diana Gabaldon, master of historical romance and time-travel adventure.
Themes: love across time, loyalty, cultural collision, survival, the pull between two worlds.
Blend meticulous historical detail with sweeping romance and adventure.
A protagonist torn between eras, navigating danger with intelligence, medical knowledge, and fierce will.
Stories are epic in scope — wars, voyages, political intrigue — but anchored in an enduring love story.

## Scene

Write in Diana Gabaldon's literary style:
- Rich, immersive prose that recreates historical periods through all five senses
- First-person narration with a sharp, witty, modern sensibility in a historical setting
- Passionate romantic scenes that are frank, tender, and character-driven
- Detailed period accuracy: medicine, food, clothing, battle tactics, social customs
- Humor woven through even the most dramatic scenes
- Multiple genres blended seamlessly: romance, adventure, mystery, and historical fiction
