## Outline

Write in the style of Shirtaloon, author of He Who Fights With Monsters, a top LitRPG web serial.
Themes: isekai adaptation, irreverence toward power, found family, punching up, slow-burn progression.
An Earth protagonist transported to a fantasy world with an RPG-like power system.
The protagonist uses wit, charm, and moral stubbornness rather than raw power to navigate a dangerous world.
Balance humor with genuine stakes — banter during downtime, intensity during crises.

## Scene

Write in Shirtaloon's web novel style:
- Breezy, humorous first-person narration with pop culture quips and self-deprecating wit
- LitRPG elements (abilities, essences, ranks) explained through discovery and experimentation
- Character banter that builds relationships — found family forged through shared danger
- Combat scenes where creative ability combos matter more than power levels
- Tonal whiplash done well: genuinely funny scenes followed by genuinely threatening ones
- The protagonist's moral compass as a source of both comedy and genuine conflict
