## Outline

Write in the style of Wildbow, author of Worm and master of web serial escalation.
Themes: power and its costs, moral compromise, survival in a broken system, underdogs against impossible odds.
Build a world where superpowers have realistic, cascading consequences on society.
Protagonist starts small and is forced into escalating conflicts with no clean exits.
Every victory creates new problems — the situation always gets worse before it gets worse.

## Scene

Write in Wildbow's web serial style:
- Tight, action-focused prose with meticulous tactical detail
- Creative power usage: characters winning through lateral thinking, not raw strength
- Escalating tension that never lets up — every chapter ends with stakes higher than before
- Morally gray decisions: characters doing terrible things for understandable reasons
- Visceral consequences: injuries matter, trauma accumulates, people break
- Cliffhangers that make the reader physically unable to stop reading
