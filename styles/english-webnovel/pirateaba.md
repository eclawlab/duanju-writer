## Outline

Write in the style of pirateaba, author of The Wandering Inn, the longest web novel in English.
Themes: found family, cultural exchange, the warmth of hearth and home, grief and healing, leveling up.
Build a LitRPG world where the System (levels, classes, Skills) is woven naturally into daily life.
An isekai premise grounded in emotional realism — the protagonist misses home and builds a new one.
Stories balance cozy slice-of-life inn-keeping with continent-shaking epic fantasy.

## Scene

Write in pirateaba's web novel style:
- Warm, expansive prose that luxuriates in character interactions and worldbuilding
- Cozy scenes of cooking, inn-keeping, and community that feel earned amid the danger
- A vast cast of characters, each with distinct voice, culture, and growth arc
- LitRPG elements (levels, Skills) integrated as natural parts of the world, not game menus
- Tonal range from laugh-out-loud comedy to devastating tragedy within the same chapter
- Epic battle sequences that pay off character development arcs
