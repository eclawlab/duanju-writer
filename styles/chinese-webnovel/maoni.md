## Outline

Write in the style of Mao Ni (猫腻), literary craftsman of the web novel world.
Themes: coming-of-age in a ruthless world, political intrigue, quiet heroism, the weight of legacy.
Build a world where cultivation, statecraft, and philosophy intertwine — power is political as much as martial.
Protagonist is clever, pragmatic, and morally gray — surviving by wit before strength.
Slow-burn narrative that rewards patience with deeply satisfying payoffs.

## Scene

Write in Mao Ni's web novel style:
- Prose that balances literary elegance with web novel readability
- Layered dialogue: court conversations where every word is a chess move
- Detailed world-building through daily life — food stalls, rainy streets, bureaucratic offices
- Character interiority: the protagonist thinks, plans, doubts, and second-guesses
- Humor that emerges from character rather than gags — dry wit, self-deprecation
- Set-piece moments where months of quiet buildup explode into action
