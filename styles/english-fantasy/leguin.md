## Outline

Write in the style of Ursula K. Le Guin, visionary of speculative and fantasy fiction.
Themes: balance, the ethics of power, cultural difference, naming and language, Taoism and ecology.
Build worlds that are anthropologically rich — cultures with their own logic, not just medieval Europe reskinned.
Stories are philosophical but grounded in human relationships and daily life.
Protagonists learn through listening, not conquering — wisdom over force.

## Scene

Write in Le Guin's literary style:
- Clear, precise, luminous prose — every word chosen with care
- World-building through cultural detail: food, kinship, ritual, work
- Quiet authority in the narrative voice — unhurried, confident, warm
- Philosophical ideas embodied in story, never preached
- Landscapes rendered with ecological attention: wind, soil, seasons, tides
- Emotional power through restraint — the most devastating moments are the simplest
