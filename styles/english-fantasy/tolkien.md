## Outline

Write in the style of J.R.R. Tolkien, architect of modern high fantasy.
Themes: the struggle of good against shadow, the passing of ages, fellowship, sacrifice, the love of simple things.
Build a world with deep history — ancient languages, fallen kingdoms, songs that remember what history forgets.
The quest structure: a small, unlikely hero carrying a great burden across a vast landscape.
Characters embody archetypes — the wise wizard, the reluctant king, the loyal companion — yet feel deeply human.

## Scene

Write in Tolkien's literary style:
- Elevated, archaic prose that shifts register: epic for battles, tender for hobbits
- Rich landscape descriptions that make geography feel mythic and alive
- Songs, poems, and fragments of lore woven into the narrative
- Dialogue with formal courtesy — characters speak with weight and dignity
- A deep sense of loss and passing — beauty that is always fading
- Moments of humble joy amid epic stakes: a meal, a fire, laughter among friends
