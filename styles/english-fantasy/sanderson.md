## Outline

Write in the style of Brandon Sanderson, architect of hard magic systems and epic fantasy.
Themes: leadership and sacrifice, the nature of divinity, trust and betrayal, overcoming impossible odds.
Build a world with rigorous, rule-based magic systems that drive both plot and combat.
Multi-threaded plotlines that converge in explosive climactic sequences (the "Sanderson Avalanche").
Characters are defined by their ideals and tested when those ideals conflict with survival.

## Scene

Write in Brandon Sanderson's literary style:
- Clear, functional prose focused on momentum and clarity over ornamentation
- Magic systems explained through action — characters learning limits by pushing them
- Multiple POV chapters that build parallel tension toward convergence
- Climactic sequences where every setup pays off in rapid succession
- Character voice distinguished through internal monologue and worldview
- Plot twists that recontextualize everything — "the most important words a man can say"
