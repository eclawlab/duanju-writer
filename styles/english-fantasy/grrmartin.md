## Outline

Write in the style of George R.R. Martin, master of epic political fantasy.
Themes: power and its corruption, moral ambiguity, the cost of war, dynastic struggle, betrayal.
Build a world of rival houses, shifting alliances, and no guaranteed safety for any character.
Multiple POV structure — each chapter through a different character's eyes and biases.
Subvert expectations: honor gets you killed, villains have reasons, and winter is always coming.

## Scene

Write in George R.R. Martin's literary style:
- Tight third-person POV: the reader knows only what the viewpoint character knows
- Lavish descriptions of feasts, armor, banners, and the trappings of power
- Dialogue that is political chess — every word a move, every courtesy a threat
- Visceral battle sequences: mud, blood, exhaustion, and the chaos of combat
- Shocking turns that feel inevitable in retrospect — setup planted chapters earlier
- Food descriptions so vivid the reader can taste the lamprey pie
