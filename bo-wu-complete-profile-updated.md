# Bo Wu (吴波 / Wu Bo) - Complete Career Profile

## Education
- **Bachelor's Degree:** Automation, Tsinghua University, China (1988)
- **Master's Degree:** Computer Science, Western Michigan University, USA (1992)

## Career Summary
Bo Wu is a prominent Chinese serial entrepreneur with over 20 years of experience in high-tech and internet sectors. He has founded or co-founded at least **9 companies**, with **5 early ventures achieving successful exits** through acquisitions or listings. His entrepreneurial journey spans e-commerce, software, real estate tech, biotech, and Web3/blockchain.

### 5. Lashou Group, Inc. / Lashou.com (拉手网) (2009-2013)
**Location:** China

**Role:** Co-Founder & CEO (2009-2011), continued until acquisition in 2013

**Company Profile:**
- **China's largest group-buying platform** (modeled after Groupon)
- Launched in 2010, rapidly expanded to serve **over 200 cities**
- Location-based services with social features
- Popular categories: cosmetics, beauty, dining, entertainment

**Operational Scale (Peak):**
- **Employees:** 1,500
- **Daily Visitors:** 1.5 million unique visitors
- **Revenue:** RMB 11.5 million in December 2010 alone
- **Cities Served:** 200+ cities in China
- Operated call centers, logistics, and "Lashou Experience Shops"

**Funding:**
- **Total Raised:** $166 million (highest for any group-buying company in China at the time)
- **Series C (2011):** $110 million led by Milestone Capital
- **Investors:** GSR Ventures, Norwest Venture Partners, Tenaya Capital, and others

**Recognition:**
- Winner at **ChinaMode Awards 2011** for innovative contributions to China's tech landscape

**Exit:**
- **Acquired by HuTuSanBao (Sanpower Group)** in 2013
- Publicly traded Chinese company acquisition
- ✅ **Successful Exit**

**Legacy:**
- Helped popularize group-buying model in China
- Competed against Groupon and inspired wave of similar platforms
- Contributed to China's O2O (online-to-offline) commerce boom
